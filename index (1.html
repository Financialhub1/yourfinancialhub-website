About Your Financial Hub

Founded in 2024, Your Financial Hub is a Baltimore-based financial firm dedicated to helping individuals, families, and small businesses build wealth with clarity and confidence.

I'm Tridonna Bullock, a financial educator, business strategist, and founder of the #IInvest movement. With over 15 years of experience in accounting, investing, and financial literacy, I help people build wealth through knowledge, confidence, and community.

Whether you're a business owner trying to scale, an adult wanting to create or preserve wealth, or a school looking to empower students, we've got a program for you.
